Recipe

Chocolate Cake

"Classic Chocolate Cake Made Easy. A fast and easy recipe of the classic chocolate cake. Enjoy!"

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Dark Chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Butter: 200g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instructions

1

 Melt the chocolate and the butter together

2

 Mix the sugar, eggs and flour

3

 Mix everything together to make cool stylish dough

4

 Bake for 30 min at 350F.
